COVID Death Rate by State

Pre-Vaccine

03-20-2020 --- 12-14-2020

Before vaccines were available, the death rate was strongly correlated to the population density.

The 3 highest death rates were seen in the higher density states of New York, New Jersey and Massachuetts.

Correlation between death rate and population density: Strongly Positive (r = 0.55)

Correlation between death rate and margin: Essentially Zero (r = 0.05)
