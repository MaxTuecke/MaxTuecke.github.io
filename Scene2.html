COVID Death Rate by State

First 6 Months Post-Vaccine

12-15-2020 --- 06-14-2021

The death rate is no longer strongly correlated with population density, but is moderately negatively correlated with vaccination rate.

The 3 highest death rates were seen in the lower density states of Arizona, Alabama and Oaklahoma, all of which had lower vaccination rates and lower margins for Biden.

Correlation between death rate and margin: Mildly Negative (r = -0.24)

Correlation between death rate and population density: Mildly Posiitive (0.19)

Correlation between death rate and vaccination rate: Moderately Negative (-0.38)
